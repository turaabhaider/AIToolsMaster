Inquiry Handling and Response Policy

We value every message we receive and strive to connect you with the right expert as quickly as possible. To ensure efficiency, please review the categories below so we can prioritize your request appropriately. Our team of reviewers, developers, and content specialists are dedicated to providing thorough and timely support.

1. Technical Support & Tutorial Feedback

If you're experiencing an issue with a specific tool we've reviewed, or if you found an error in one of our **tutorials**, please select the "Technical Support" subject line (hypothetically, if the form had one). Our developers use this feedback to immediately verify and update our guides. **Standard Response Time:** 12-24 hours. Be sure to include the specific tool name (e.g., DALL·E, Zapier) and the link to the tutorial in question.

2. Partnership and Advertising Inquiries

AIToolsMaster is open to collaboration with SaaS providers, AI developers, and affiliate marketing partners that align with our mission of high-quality tools. Please use this channel for media kits, sponsored content requests, or partnership discussions. We maintain strict editorial independence, and all partnership requests are routed through our business development team. **Standard Response Time:** 24-48 hours. Please outline your proposal clearly in the message body.

3. Review Methodology and Editorial Questions

Questions regarding our **review scores, testing methodology, or editorial policies** should be directed here. Transparency is a core value, and we are happy to explain our 40-hour deep-dive process and how we maintain objectivity. This helps us reinforce our **Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness (E-A-T)**—principles vital to our site’s integrity.

4. General Feedback and Suggestions

We love hearing from our community! If you have suggestions for a new tool we should review, a tutorial we should create, or general feedback about the site's design or user experience, this is the place. While these messages are read by the entire team, they may take slightly longer to receive a dedicated response. We promise your input shapes the future of AIToolsMaster.
